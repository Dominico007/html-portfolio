The Greatest TV Series According to Dominico

My Top 5 All-time Series

1. Peaky Blinders

Thomas Shelby was just absolutely sensational in this. Good plot that should have had more seasons.

2. Vikings

Ragnar was just amazing overall; I also loved some of his sons, Bjorn Ironside, Ivar the Boneless, Ubbe and Hvitserk. Then my heart would always go to Lagertha and Bishop Heahmurd.

3. Friends

No sitcom could be anymore the perfect than this. It wasn't just comedy, but comedy with a wonderful storyline.

4. How I Met Your Mother

Absolutely sensational are just the right words for it. Every chaacter was just wonderful.

5. The Office

All I can say is, "lived it, loved it". A series after my own heart.
